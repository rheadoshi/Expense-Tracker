## About the Project

The Expense Tracker is an Android application designed to help user manage their finances by tracking income and expenses. Users can add, edit, and view their financial transactions and see their spending patterns through intuitive charts on the dashboard.

## Features

- **Add Income and Expenses:** Easily add details of income and expenses, including the source and amount.
- **Edit Transactions:** Modify existing transactions to keep your records accurate.
- **Dashboard with Charts:** Visualize your financial data with charts and graphs for better understanding and analysis.
- **Transaction History:** View a detailed history of all your transactions.
